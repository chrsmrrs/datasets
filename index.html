TUD Datasets

A collection of benchmark data sets for graph classification and regression.

Learn more


This page contains collected benchmark data sets for the evaluation of graph kernels and graph neural networks. The data sets were collected by Kristian Kersting, Nils M. Kriege, Christopher Morris, Petra Mutzel, and Marion Neumann with partial support of the German Science Foundation (DFG) within the Collaborative Research Center SFB 876 “Providing Information by Resource-Constrained Data Analysis”, project A6 “Resource-efficient Graph Mining”.
